Reward System Description

Technician:-

  1. Technicians earn rewards based on the time taken to resolve issues assigned to them.
  2. If the time taken to resolve an issue is less than 30 minutes, the technician earns 4 rewards.
  3. If the time taken is between 30 minutes and 2 hours, the technician earns 2 rewards.
  4. If the time taken exceeds 4 hours, the technician earns 1 reward.
  5. These rewards are accumulated over time as the technician resolves issues, and they can be used as incentives or recognition for their work.

Operator:-

  1. Operators may also earn rewards, typically based on their performance in managing assets or handling operational tasks efficiently.
  2. The reward system for operators could be tailored to incentivize actions that contribute to the smooth functioning of operations, such as minimizing downtime, optimizing resource usage, or improving overall efficiency.
  3. For example, operators may earn rewards for achieving specific performance targets, completing tasks within deadlines, or implementing innovative solutions that enhance productivity.
